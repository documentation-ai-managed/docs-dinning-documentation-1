## Overview

Get up and running with ดีเจ Dinning Documentation in minutes. Follow these steps to create your account, set up a new documentation project, and publish your first page. This guide assumes you have a web browser ready.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Bookmark your dashboard after login for quick access to your projects.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Start by signing up for ดีเจ Dinning Documentation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the signup page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the [ดีเจ Dinning signup page](https://app.djdinning.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details" icon="user">
    Provide your email address, create a strong password, and complete any verification steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a New Documentation Project

Once logged in, create your first project to organize your docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the dashboard" icon="home">
    From the sidebar, click **New Project**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your project" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a project name like `My App Docs` and select a starter template if desired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings" icon="settings">
    Choose your preferred language and theme. ดีเจ Dinning supports custom domains for production.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project" icon="plus">
    Click **Create** to generate your documentation space.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Edit Your First Page

Populate your project with content using the built-in editor.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Visual Editor" icon="edit">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create a page">
        In your project, click **New Page** and select **Markdown**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Edit content">
        Use the WYSIWYG editor to add headings, lists, and images. Preview changes in real-time.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Code Editor" icon="code">
    Switch to the code view for advanced MDX features.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
```mdx
---
title: Welcome
description: Your first page.
---

## Hello World

This is your **first documentation page** in ดีเจ Dinning.
```
```markdown
# Welcome

This is your **first documentation page** in ดีเจ Dinning.
```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="info">
  Save frequently with `Cmd+S` (`Ctrl+S` on Windows) to avoid losing work.
</Callout>
